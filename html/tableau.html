Profits are faring decently well in the 50 states, only a few states such as California and New York are very productive. The states that are not productive don't loose more than the profitable states can offset.

California (the highest-profit state) has fared well in the past two years, although there are lots of ups and downs, it is definitely trending upwards.

Within California, profits in each product category are good. Office supplies is leading, followed closely by technology. Furniture is making a profit but lagging behind the other two categories.

In California, the most profitable category of office supplies forecasted through November 2015 shows a steady incline of sales.
